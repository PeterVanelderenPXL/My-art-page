Hyperrealism

"The Masterpiece"

(100x70cm - bristol)

This is a project I did many years ago for a hairdresser in Belgium.
It is fully made with graphite and carchoal and took about 3 months of intens work to finish.

Before i started the project we agreed that we would value the artwork when finished.

He never paid after receiving the artwork.

"Scream in the dark"

(pencil H, B, B4, B8 and carchoal)

Sometimes in life you don't feel so good or things don't go your way.
This is an artwork I made in a period of anger and depression.
It represents the fear and darkness of oneself.

02

Anamorphosis

"Pulley"

For 11 years I worked at a big automotive company where they make these "pulley's" for the asian market.

I used to design equipment for measuring these pulleys and in order to make it interesting i made an artwork out of it.

For this piece I combined realism with Anamorphosis to create a Realistic 3D look.

03

Illustrations

"CEO presentation"

These are some illustrations I made for the CEO of a big automotive company.

After some corporate struggles the board wanted to give a mental boost to the employees and company culture.

The CEO asked me to create supporting visuals in his presentation. the visuals had to create a feeling for the tough situation at the time and also show the corporate goals

the illustrations represent the difficulties and goals we had to overcome as a company.
The future was lying ahead of us.
